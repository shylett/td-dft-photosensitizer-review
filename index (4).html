TD-DFT Review: Photosensitizer Modeling for Photodynamic Therapy

This site presents a structured literature synthesis and conceptual simulation workflow focused on time-dependent density functional theory (TD-DFT) applications in photodynamic therapy (PDT).

Research Overview

The analysis includes electronic transition modeling, triplet state energetics, singlet oxygen yield predictions, and molecular delivery mechanisms, grounded in TD-DFT simulations.
